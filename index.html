Raft Wars is a fun, level-based shooting game created by Martijn Kunst, where you and your brother Simon will need to defend your treasure from enemies of all kinds! Initially armed with your raft and only some tennis balls, you'll need to focus on your aim and power in order to defeat vikings, pirates, gangs, and more!

Hit your opponent to knock them off their raft or wear down their health until they fall in! Earn coins by making strategic shots in order to unlock grenades, rockets, and raft upgrades.
